Rolling Arm Bar w/ Screwdriver Rotation
Rolling Arm Bar w/ Screwdriver Rotation
2 sets, 8 reps
 2 8 --:--

This drill works miracles for building hip internal rotation mobility, but the details can be a bit tricky. It’s ok if you don’t get it down day 1.

Follow the directions in the video but I want you to especially think about the following tips when you perform the exercise.

The leg that is down on the ground must be actively pushed into the floor throughout the entire exercise. Don’t lose pressure in that leg or you won’t get the hip mobility benefits.

Breath in through the nose as you roll over, and out through the mouth when you roll back to the starting position.

Keep the knee that is down fully locked out and the foot dorsiflexed (toes pulled up towards your head).

Make sure the additional weight is light, between 10 and 20 lbs.
