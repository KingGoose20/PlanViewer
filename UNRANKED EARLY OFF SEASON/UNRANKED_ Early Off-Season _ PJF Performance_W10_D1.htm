Short Corner Rhythm Shooting
2 sets, 8 reps, 01:00 rest
 2 8 01:00

Aim for 8 makes, then rest until you feel fresh and go again. (2 sets = 16 total makes)
-Start in the "short corner" about 12-15 feet from the basket. Shoot from one corner, jog to get your rebound and go to the other corner, get your feet squared to the basket and shoot. Repeat until you've made 10 shots total, then take a break for 5 free throws. Complete the recommended sets.

Rolling Arm Bar w/ Screwdriver Rotation
Rolling Arm Bar w/ Screwdriver Rotation
2 sets, 8 reps
 2 8 --:--

This drill works miracles for building hip internal rotation mobility, but the details can be a bit tricky. Its ok if you dont get it down day 1.

Follow the directions in the video but I want you to especially think about the following tips when you perform the exercise.

The leg that is down on the ground must be actively pushed into the floor throughout the entire exercise. Dont lose pressure in that leg or you wont get the hip mobility benefits.

Breath in through the nose as you roll over, and out through the mouth when you roll back to the starting position.

Keep the knee that is down fully locked out and the foot dorsiflexed (toes pulled up towards your head).

Make sure the additional weight is light, between 10 and 20 lbs.

High Volume Freestyle Spot Shooting
High Volume Freestyle Spot Shooting
5 sets, 30 reps
 5 30 --:--

We are aiming for 150 makes total. Each set will be at a new spot (corner, wing, top of key, opposite wing, opposite corner). It will be a catch and shoot 3 followed by a catch and rip 1 dribble pullup. If you miss one, repeat that shot. We are aiming for 30 total makes at each spot, so thats 15 3's and 15 mid range.
Make 30 total shots from 5 spots (150 makes total). You will make a 3 point shot, then catch and rip right mid range, then catch and rip left mid range. Repeat that cycle 10 times to make a total of 30 shots. Once you get comfortable, start to freestyle a bit and use different footwork, different moves, etc. Really use your visualization and make it feel like real game reps!
