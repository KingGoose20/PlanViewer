Hamstring Slider Progression
Hamstring Slider Progression
3 sets, 6 reps
 3 6 --:--

If you have access to a regular hamstring machine curl, that is a great option as well.

Level 1 = 1 leg stationary in a bridge position while other leg goes through full range of motion curl. Start with about 50% pressure on the non working leg for added support, gradually work towards less pressure on that non working leg. Once you can do less than about 20% of pressure on that non working leg (80% pressure through the curl leg), move on to level 2.

Level 2 = one leg bridges while other leg curls, switch the legs each rep. 1 set = completion of recommended reps on each leg. To make this variation more challenging you can add a dumbbell or medicine ball right above the hip and hold it down with your hands.

Level 3 = true 1 leg 3 second lowering. Hips stay down and put pressure into floor on the way back up. Bridge up again, and repeat. You can add weight just above the hip to make this more challenging.

-You should feel this exercise all in the hamstrings. If you feel it in the lower back you may be hyperextending the lower back too much instead of extending the hip.

Inverted Row Progression
3 sets, 8 reps
 3 8 --:--

Make the exercise easier by starting more vertical. As you get stronger, gradually move your feet lower to make your body more horizontal to the floor.

If don't have access to a rack or TRX straps to perform this exercise, you can use a kneeling rotational row. Click "alternate exercise" to view.

High Volume Freestyle Spot Shooting
High Volume Freestyle Spot Shooting
5 sets, 30 reps
 5 30 --:--

We are aiming for 150 makes total. Each set will be at a new spot (corner, wing, top of key, opposite wing, opposite corner). It will be a catch and shoot 3 followed by a catch and rip 1 dribble pullup. If you miss one, repeat that shot. We are aiming for 30 total makes at each spot, so thats 15 3's and 15 mid range.
Make 30 total shots from 5 spots (150 makes total). You will make a 3 point shot, then catch and rip right mid range, then catch and rip left mid range. Repeat that cycle 10 times to make a total of 30 shots. Once you get comfortable, start to freestyle a bit and use different footwork, different moves, etc. Really use your visualization and make it feel like real game reps!

Hamstring Slider Progression
Hamstring Slider Progression
3 sets, 6 reps
 3 6 --:--

If you have access to a regular hamstring machine curl, that is a great option as well.

Level 1 = 1 leg stationary in a bridge position while other leg goes through full range of motion curl. Start with about 50% pressure on the non working leg for added support, gradually work towards less pressure on that non working leg. Once you can do less than about 20% of pressure on that non working leg (80% pressure through the curl leg), move on to level 2.

Level 2 = one leg bridges while other leg curls, switch the legs each rep. 1 set = completion of recommended reps on each leg. To make this variation more challenging you can add a dumbbell or medicine ball right above the hip and hold it down with your hands.

Level 3 = true 1 leg 3 second lowering. Hips stay down and put pressure into floor on the way back up. Bridge up again, and repeat. You can add weight just above the hip to make this more challenging.

-You should feel this exercise all in the hamstrings. If you feel it in the lower back you may be hyperextending the lower back too much instead of extending the hip.
